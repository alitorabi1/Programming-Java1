Using the order of precedence rules,
3 * 7 - 6 + 2 * 5 / 4 + 6
means the following:
(((3 * 7) - 6) + ((2 * 5) / 4 )) + 6
= ((21 - 6) + (10 / 4)) + 6 (Evaluate *)
= ((21 - 6) + 2) + 6 (Evaluate /. Note that this is an integer division.)
= (15 + 2) + 6 (Evaluate -)
= 17 + 6 (Evaluate first +)
= 23 (Evaluate +)
